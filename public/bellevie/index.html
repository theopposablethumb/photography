Our commitment to care

At Bellevie our vision is to create an environment where people who provide and receive care thrive together.

Just enough support

Everyon is different so our Wellbeing Support Workers are skilled in adjusting to meet the needs of those we support, while maintaining their independence as much as possible . We take our time to really get to know people and build a relationship based on trust.

Per Person not per hour

Unlike other care companies, here Bellevie we provide a simple easy to understand fixed price package rather than a price based on an hourly rate. This allows us to focus on your loved ones and not on the clock.

Valuing our people

We value our colleagues and want to attract top professionals. This is why we pay our Wellbeing Support Workers annual salaries and not by the visit. The better the team, the better the quality of care.

Going Beyond Care

We begin with an initial conversation to understand your immediate needs and together, we create a plan to support those needs. But we don’t stop there. We also take the time to get to know and understand your history, interests, and what’s important to you.

How we can support you

Each person we support is unique, and therefore each of our care packages are individually tailored to suit the needs of the individual.
