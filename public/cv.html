Employment History

UX Designer, Front End Developer – BelleVieCare.co.uk - September 2020 – Current

BelleVieCare are a start-up business reinventing social care, and introducing self-management principles to remotely distributed teams of carers.

I was asked to join the team as a Business Analyst to research and develop a strategy for a time scheduling system for self-managing teams of careers, typically users with a low exposure to digital products in late middle-age. The system is required to replace a complicated Google Sheet supported by complex Python functions, which is prone to manual error and often breaks due to user input.

I conducted several user and stakeholder interviews, and collated my findings into a set of process maps and proposed user journeys which I used to product requirements. Since BelleVie is a small start-up I took the opportunity to develop user persona’s and design a UI in Sketch, which I presented in regular show and tell sessions and individually to potential users. Feedback was then incorporated into interactive prototypes which I developed using ReactJS, which I then took into formal user testing.

BelleVie soon recruited a full development team, with a full product roadmap and backlog informed by my research and testing. As a collaborative team we have gone on to develop a working Minimum Lovable Product in 3 months which is currently in a live trial with a Care Team. As a team we are continuously iterating and learning, however initial results have shown a small increase in time efficiency within the team.

I have also supported BelleVie analysing and simplifying manual and complex back-office processes for payroll, invoicing, and recruitment.
